Welcome to my website!


I’m Sebastian Sensale, a Bioengineering PhD candidate from Dr. Chia Chang’s group at the Aerospace and Mechanical Engineering Department, University of Notre Dame. I work on the development of biosensors for early diagnosis of DNA-related diseases. My interests are placed in the intersection of fundamental and applied research, as I focus on the use of theoretical and computational models to better understand how different impedance-based biosensors work.
